Best places for tourists to explore the Philippines


The Philippines is a archapeligo located in the Pacific Ocean near the equator that has 7,641 islands, it is mainly divided into 3 areas which is Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. It is also a country that gained a lot of popularity for its Breath taking, and fun experiences, including thier culture. There are many fun places tourists can explore from famous landmarks such as churches, in other Philippine places there are famous mountains and beaches or islands in some provinces. The Philippines is a Tropical and maritime country which has abundant rainfalls including typhoons, and high tempratures on summer days.



These are the 3 best Philippines places for tourists to explore:


Chocolate Hills

Chocolate Hills also called the "Eighth wonder of the world" is the Philippines third geological monument known for its scenery view. Chocolate hills may not be the tallest mountain, it ranges 100 ft and 165ft tall It is Located in Bicol Region.

Intramuros

Intramuros was also known as the "Walled City", In this place there are old churches and you can visit the Fort Santiago where the Rizal Shrine (Museo ni Rizal) is located. It was built by Miguel Lopez de Legaspi back in 1571 during the Spanish colonial period.

El Nido

El Nido is one of the cleanest island in Palawan with the clearest water lagoons where it is safe for tourists to explore with fun activities such as swimming, and cliff climbing.
